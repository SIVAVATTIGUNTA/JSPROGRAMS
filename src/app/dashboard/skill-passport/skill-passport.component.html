Applicant Skill Passport


What is Skill Passport?

Skill passport is a new concept from hirextra to design your own skill passport like a portfolio. Skill passport will design Applicant skills in passport model.


Why Skill Passport?

Skill passport will give a more information about Applicant responsibilities and abilities. Applicant can able to share his skill passport report via social networks like Facebook, Linkedin, Google plus or whatsapp.


How to Generate Skill Passport?

By uploading Applicant resume our business algorithm will extract information from resume and display the result in passbook page animation view.
